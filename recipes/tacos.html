Tacos

Tacos have been one of my favorite meals since childhood. This recipe has evolved over time into a delicious and easy to make dinner, any night of the week.

Ingredients

  • x1 Onion
  • Ground Beef
  • Your favorite taco seasoning (I recommend Frontera taco seasoning)
  • Rosarita refried beans
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • White or yellow corn tortillas
  • Canned black olives
  • chopped garlic
  • La Victoria green taco sauce
  • Avocado

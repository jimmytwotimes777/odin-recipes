Tacos

Description

Tacos have been one of my favorite meals since childhood. This recipe has evolved over time into a delicious and easy to make dinner, any night of the week.


Ingredients

  • x1 Onion
  • Ground Beef
  • Your favorite taco seasoning (I recommend Frontera taco seasoning)
  • Rosarita refried beans
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • White or yellow corn tortillas
  • Canned black olives
  • chopped garlic
  • La Victoria green taco sauce
  • Avocado
  • Cumin, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Chop onion and saute in olive oil. Once soft, add large spoonful of garlic.
  2. Add ground beef and cook till brown. While browning, add cumin and salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Add taco (Frontera) taco seasoning.
  4. Add refried beans, olive oil, and a handful of cheese to a separate pot and cook on low-med heat until beans are soft.
  5. In small pan, add enough olive oil to top over a flat corn tortilla. Once warm, cook tortillas until semi-crispy
  6. Add cooked meat and beans, olives, green sauce, and cheddar to tortilla.
  7. Cut avocado into thin slices and top tacos if you like.
  8. A flat, fried, and then salted "chip" along side the tacos is always a hit.
  9. Enjoy!

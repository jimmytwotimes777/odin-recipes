Burritos

Description

Burritos are a quick and tasty meal to make for the family after a long day at work when you don't have the energy for a big production that is sure to leave even the pickiest eaters satisfied.


Ingredients

  • x1 Onion
  • Ground Beef
  • Tajin seasoning
  • Rosarita refried beans
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Burrito size flour tortillas
  • Canned black olives
  • chopped garlic
  • 8oz can of tomato paste
  • Green sauce, or salsa depending on preference
  • Cumin, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Chop onion and saute in olive oil. Once soft, add large spoonful of garlic.
  2. Add ground beef and cook till brown. While browning, add cumin and salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Add Tajin seasoning to taste.
  4. Add tomato paste and 1 can of water. Stir and season to taste
  5. Add refried beans, olive oil, and a handful of cheese to a separate pot and cook on low-med heat until beans are soft.
  6. Add cooked meat and beans, olives, green sauce or salsa, and cheddar to tortilla and wrap tightly.
  7. Enjoy!
